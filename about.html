About Swabhumi Entertainment

Swabhumi Entertainment is an independent film production company based in Kolkata, dedicated to creating auteur-driven cinema rooted in Indian socio-cultural realities. The company focuses on developing visually immersive and emotionally layered narratives that explore human vulnerability, identity, and existential conflict.

With a minimalist and realist approach, Swabhumi Entertainment collaborates with distinctive creative voices to produce films that balance regional authenticity with universal resonance. Its work is designed for the global arthouse circuit, with a strong emphasis on international film festivals, co-production markets, and curated distribution platforms.
